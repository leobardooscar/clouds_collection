ABOUT THIS COLLECTION

In meteorology, a phenomenon observed in the atmosphere or on the surface of the Earth is known as a meteor. It may be a form of precipitation, a suspension or a deposit of liquid or solid particles.

The present collection consists of meteors observed from 2010 to 2024. Sighting point: Guadalajara Metropolitan Area (Northwest zone). Western Mexico region (20.6752° N, 103.3473° W).
